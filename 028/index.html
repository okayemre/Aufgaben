Guess the Number, Win the Points!

    Game Instructions

  • Enter how many points you want to win (between 1 and 50).
  • Choose a number between 1 and 100.
  • Click the Play button.
  • The game will generate a random number between 1 and 100.
  • If your number is close enough to the random number (within your chosen point range), you win!
  • Otherwise, you lose. Try again!
